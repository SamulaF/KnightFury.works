About F1 in Schools

F1 in Schools is an international STEM competition for secondary school students (aged 1119), in which groups of 36 students design and manufacture a miniature F1 car using CAD/CAM and CAE design tools.

Our Legacy

Since 2016, Knight Fury has been proudly representing India in the F1 in Schools competition. Our journey is filled with passion, dedication, and remarkable achievements. Here are some highlights of our past successes:

  • 2016 - Present: Participating annually in F1 in Schools, making it to the nationals every season.
  • Best Pit Display: Awarded the Best Pit Display at the Regional Level.
  • Team Identity Award: Nominated for the prestigious Team Identity Award.
  • Knockout Championship: Landed in the top 4 during the Knockout Championship at the Regional Level.
  • National Finals 2023-24: Competed in the National Finals with pride and excellence.
  • Knockout Champions: Previous wins include titles like Knockout Champions and Best Verbal Presentation.
  • Best Engineered Car: Recognized for outstanding engineering in the design of our F1 car.

Our teams dedication and passion have driven us to achieve milestones each season. We're looking forward to building on this legacy with the support of our partners.
